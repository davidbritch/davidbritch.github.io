Hilo C++ has been localized into 11 different languages. Including the American English original, that means it’s available in 12 languages. Here’s the list:

  • Hilo in American English
  • Hilo in Russian
  • Hilo in French
  • Hilo in Chinese
  • Hilo in Taiwanese
  • Hilo in Japanese
  • Hilo in Spanish
  • Hilo in German
  • Hilo in Portuguese
  • Hilo in Korean
  • Hilo in Italian
  • Hilo in Ukrainian
